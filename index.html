what i do

01/ UX[ user experience ]

People are always the priority. I seek a deeper understanding of a product’s intent and its user’s needs to strategize the bigger picture and develop full product experiences — feature definition, user stories, wireframes, mock-ups, final UI designs — that delight the end user while aligning with business rules and KPIs.

02/ UI[ user interface ]

A pretty face will only get you so far. I create seamless interfaces and flexible design systems from adaptable components that are efficient for development and allow products to scale. My strong attention to detail keeps elements consistent and familiar, guiding the user and preventing unexpected outcomes.

03/ PM[ project mgmtmanagement ]

A chain is only as strong as its weakest link. I collaborate and build relationships with my product, engineering, and business team stakeholders to establish a clear understanding of project goals, timeline, and contingencies. By setting appropriate expectations and delegating tasks effectively, I ensure deadlines are consistently met.
